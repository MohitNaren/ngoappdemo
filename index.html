WHO WE ARE

A Certified NGO Built on Transparency & Impact

Founded in September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit operating across 28 states of India with a network of 200+ dedicated volunteers and corporate partners.

Full Legal Compliance Section 8 registered, licensed by the Central Government of India.
Donor Tax Benefits 80G & 12A certified — all donations are fully tax-exempt.
Corporate Partnerships CSR-1 registered to collaborate with companies on impactful initiatives.
